HUNT THE SNAKE

HOW TO PLAY?

  • Click on the button below to start the game.
  • Now click on the mode of play i.e. Easy, Medium, Hard.
  • Try to click on the Snake (The snake will move randomloy in any direction).
  • Your score will be displayed on the sidebar.
  • If the score is less than 10 then you will lose the game. And if the score is greater than 10 you win the game.
  • To restart your game click on restart button below the scorecard or simply refresh the page.
